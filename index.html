Who We Are

A Launchpad for Passionate Minds

We believe that youth are not just the future—they are the key to unlocking immediate community progress. NayePankh is a beginner-friendly, student-led environment focused on giving practical experience, fostering social consciousness, and building student confidence.

Hands-on Leadership Experience

Direct Community Impact

Our Impact Focus

Core Pillars of NayePankh

We focus our efforts across five primary thematic areas to achieve comprehensive social development and empower the next generation.

Education

Promoting child literacy, establishing local study circles, and distributing learning resources to underprivileged kids.

Youth Empowerment

Creating opportunities for freshers and students to lead initiatives, build confidence, and take charge of social change.

Skill Development

Offering training in digital literacy, communication, public speaking, and project coordination to boost employability.

Social Awareness

Conducting advocacy campaigns on mental health, gender equality, environmental sustainability, and civic duties.

Community Impact

Driving rapid-response distribution drives, sanitation awareness, and micro-initiatives catering to urgent local needs.
